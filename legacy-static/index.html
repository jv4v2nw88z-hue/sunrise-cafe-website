Welcome to Sunrise Cafe

A Real Breakfast Worth Getting Up For

We're a neighborhood spot in Battle Creek doing breakfast and lunch the right way — fresh eggs, hand-cut meats, homemade French toast, and an omelet for every craving. Whether you're a regular who knows your order by heart or a first-timer looking for the best breakfast in town, you'll feel at home here.

Drop in any morning (except Tuesday — we rest) and let us take care of you. Open 6AM to 2PM, six days a week.
